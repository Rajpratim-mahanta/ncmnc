National Conference on Multidisciplinary Approaches in Networks, and Computing (NCMNC-2025)

Dibrugarh University is proud to host a National Conference aimed at advancing cross-disciplinary research and innovation in the fields of networks and computing.

The goals are to foster cross-disciplinary research and knowledge exchange among experts from diverse fields, such as computer science, engineering, mathematics, physics, chemistry, and social sciences, to drive innovative solutions in networks and computing. This platform enables researchers to share their latest findings on emerging trends, breakthroughs, and future directions while applying these technologies to address societal and industrial challenges, aligned with Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 17. Additionally, it seeks to create a supportive environment for networking, knowledge sharing, and collaboration, strengthening connections among researchers, students, and industry professionals to maximize societal impact.
